BringList

BringList is an simple Android app that reads your incoming text messages for any items that you need to buy or grab.

Free Download From Google Play Store

No More Lists

Just ask the person to send you a text starting with 'Bring' followed by comma seperated items. For example: 'Bring eggs,bread,milk'

Leave it to Us

BringList will read your incoming sms. If it starts with bring, BringList will make a list and notify you.

Just Tap

Just tap the BringList icon to access BringList. It is sticky so you cant swipe it away! Got everything you need? Just go into BringList and delete the items.
